Brand Guidance

  • Use the full name as Quantaudio Studio.
  • Keep whitespace around the wordmark and musical motif.
  • Do not recolor the wordmark without approval; use brand taupe #6F615D on light backgrounds.
  • For dark backgrounds, place the mark on a light chip or use an approved single-color version.
