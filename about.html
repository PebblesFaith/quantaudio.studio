What “QA” Means

QA denotes concise, scripture-rooted prompts that frame each session — questions to listen by, with answers grounded in the Word.

  • Anchored in a key verse and supporting biblical themes
  • Designed for prayer, study, devotion, and reflection
  • Minimal narration; the music carries the meditation
  • Clear credit line: “Music by Quantaudio Studio”
