Weekly Prompt New

“This is the way; walk in it.” — Isaiah 30:21

Where do you need God’s guidance this week? Name one small, faithful step you can take in that direction.

Study Flow

  1. Read the scripture slowly, twice.
  2. Note a word/phrase that stands out.
  3. Pray it back to God; ask for clarity.
  4. Write one action step; do it today.
